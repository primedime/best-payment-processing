Fattmerchant Review

Summary

Fattmerchant combines technology, award-winning service, and savings to deliver the best service around for merchants. For a flat monthly fee, Fattmerchant members have access to the direct cost of interchange with no markups, contracts, or hidden fees. Fattmerchant offers an innovative, all-in-one platform for merchants so they can view their data and manage their payments with ease. Fattmerchant members also have access to 24/7 human support through phone, email, or live chat. In fact, the company has an NPSscore of 70+ compared to the industry standard of -8. And with transparent pricing with no hidden fees orcontracts, merchants can rest easy. With their innovative technology, dedication to fantastic customer service, and commitment to transparent pricing, Fattmerchant is delivering the best payment experience possible to their members.

Features

Fattmerchant offers an industry-first, all-in-one integrated payment platform: Omni. Pricing

Pricing for Fattmerchant membership begins at $99 per month. Membership is determined by annual processing volume. So if a merchant processes less than $500,000 annually, they pay $99 per month. And if a business processes over $500,000 annually, they pay $199 per month.

Summary
